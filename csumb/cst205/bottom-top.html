Bottom Top Mirror

This function takes an image and flips it vertically across the horizontal center to have the appearance of the top and bottom mirroring, from the bottom half of the photo. Along with being a little tricky to make sure the math works out in the proper flips, the image results usually ended up a bit terrifying.

Code:

                  def horzBottomFlip():
                    file = pickAFile()
                    pic = makePicture(file)
                    for x in range (0, getWidth(pic)):
                      for y in range(getHeight(pic)/2, getHeight(pic)):
                        p = getPixel(pic, x, y)
                        newP = getPixel(pic,x,(getHeight(pic)- 1 - y))
                        color = getColor(p)
                        setColor(newP, color)
                    repaint(pic)
