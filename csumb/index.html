About the Program:

The CS Online degree completion program from California State University, Monterey Bay offers a unique blend of computer science theory and hands-on information technology practice. CS Online is a year-round 24 month program with fall, spring, and summer terms.

The program requires students to begin with by transferring 60 or more units, including the "Golden Four" of English Composition, Speech/Oral Communication, Critical Thinking, and College level Mathematics. This leaves the program able to focus on a set core, concentration, and final major learning outcomes (MLOS).
